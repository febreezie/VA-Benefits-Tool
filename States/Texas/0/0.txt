General Texas Veteran Benefits
Access to Veteran Homes (Honorable Discharge Required): Rental costs are based on income.

Educational benefits of up to 150 hours tuition free college at state schools. (Applies to spouses and Dependent Children)

Employment preference with state agencies or offices. (Wartime veterans only)

State employee benefits to include potential active duty military time towards retirement.

Entrepreneurship Programs to provide tools for starting or growing a business.

On the Job training for a trade or skill of your choice for up to 2 years. 

Temporary Unemployment benefits (if unemployed by no fault of your own).

No cost medical records when they are obtained to file a claim for disability against the Department of Veteran Affairs
